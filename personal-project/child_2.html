Vareniki are also almost the most favorite food. But unlike cheesecakes, they take much longer to make if there is no cooking experience or special improvised means to speed up their preparation.

Dough

To prepare the dough, you need to mix flour, water, egg and salt. The dough must be very well kneaded. If everything is done correctly, then the dough should not stick to your hands and should be tender and slightly elastic so that it rolls out well.

Filling

To prepare the filling, you need to mix the egg, sugar and cottage cheese.

Shaping/Sculpting varenikis

It is very good if you have something that will help you roll out the dough. If not, then you will have to do it with your hands, you also need to roll out the dough to 1-2 millimeters. After you roll out the dough, you can take a glass, a mug and cut rounds with it. Next, you need to take the round in your hands and put the filling in it and blind the edges so that the filling does not come out, since in this case the vareniki will stick in the water. You should end up with something similar to the vareniki in the picture.

Cooking

You can boil the varenikis right away or put them in the freezer first. You need to put salted water like on pasta (1 teaspoon per liter) in a saucepan on the stove and bring it to a boil, pour it into varenikis. The varenikis will sink first and then float up, and you will need to cook them a little more. The dough of the boiled vareniki should not be white.

Serving

Serving varenikis is also not very difficult. You need to take the dumplings out of the pan and grease them with butter, I also recommend eating varenikis with sour cream.
