Ukraine Cuisine

In Ukrainian cuisine, there are many delicious dishes that are difficult to prepare and which are simple. I generally prefer the ones that are easy to make and taste delicious. But there are also many dishes that can be a little difficult and time consuming to prepare, but because of their taste, I sometimes cook difficult or time consuming recipes. For example dumplings or different pies. But often cooking only seems complicated at first glance, and if you follow the recipe, I'm sure everyone can cook deliciously.

Vareniki

Varenyky are more popular food among Ukrainians. They are made with potatoes, cottage cheese, berries, cabbage. Mostly they are bought in stores, but also people make them at home, but without sufficient skill it takes a long time. But despite this, I think that dumplings are worth the time spent on them. For cooking, you will need the following ingredients: flour, eggs, water, salt, sugar, cottage cheese (I like cottage cheese the most, but boiled potatoes, berries)
